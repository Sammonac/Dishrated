Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Dishrated

What is Dishrated?
Dishrated is a social food rating platform where you can photograph meals, rate them on multiple factors (taste, health, cost, time), and share discoveries with friends. Think of it as a personal food journal meets social network for food lovers.
How does the Dishrated Score work?
Our algorithm calculates a personalized score based on taste (40%), health (35%), time (15%), and cost (10%). This gives you a comprehensive rating that considers all aspects of your meal experience, not just how it tastes.
Is Dishrated free to use?
Yes! Dishrated is completely free to use. Create an account with Google and start rating your meals immediately. We believe everyone should have access to better food discovery tools.
Do I need to download an app?
No downloads required! Dishrated is a progressive web app that works perfectly in your browser on any device. Just bookmark dishrated.com/app and you're ready to go.
Can I keep my meals private?
Absolutely. You have full control over your privacy. You can keep meals private for personal tracking, share only with followers, or make them public. Your data, your choice.
Who is Dishrated for?
Anyone who loves food! Whether you're a home cook tracking your recipes, a foodie exploring restaurants, someone focused on healthy eating, or just curious about what friends are enjoying ‚Äì Dishrated helps you make better food choices.
